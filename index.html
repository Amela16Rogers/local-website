About Lost Love

Lost Love is a small business dedicated to creating beautiful, handcrafted items that resonate with emotion and nostalgia. Each piece tells a story of love, loss, and memory.

Our founder, Sarah, started this journey after losing a cherished locket. Instead of replacing it, she began creating her own jewelry and keepsakes, each imbued with personal meaning.

Today, Lost Love offers a range of handmade items from jewelry to home decor, all crafted with the same attention to detail and emotional resonance.
